HOW TO BUY Richie Inu

1

Install MetaMask

Open your Google Chrome browser and visit metamask.org. Download the MetaMask chrome extension, set up your wallet and configure the Binance network on it (more details here).
2

Get some Smart Chain ETH

As you need some Smart Chain ETH to trade on ERC-20 based DEX, here you will need to buy some Smart Chain ETH on an exchange (CEX) like Binance or Huobi.
3

Set your MetaMask wallet

Create your MetaMask wallet then withdraw your ERC-20 from the exchange to your wallet address.
4

Buy Richie Inu

Go to the UniSwap DEX and you can now swap $ETH to Richie Inu easily. Make sure to set slippage to 10 - 12% on UniSwap for the 10% reflection fee.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1 What makes Richie Token different from the other meme coins?
We think our differences are our transparency & our work to push this project as high as possible. And of course, as a $RICHIE holder, you are basically paid to hold the token.
2 How does the transaction tax work?
Richie Token has a 0% buy, 10% sell tax that is redistributed instantly and gaslessly among ALL holders, proportional to their holdings.
3Why can't I see my Richie Inu tokens in Metamask?
You have to add a custom token in Metamask :

- Click on the "ADD TOKEN" button
- Select "Custom Token" tab
- Paste the contract address from the first step to the "Token Address" field: TBA
- Click the "NEXT" button
- Click the "ADD TOKENS" button
- You are done.
4Why do I get an error when I try to swap Richie Inu on a DEX?
It happens during a swap, when the sender transfers Richie Inu tokens to the swap contract, which gets 10% less tokens than expected. To allow the swap to happen, make sure to set slippage to at least 6-7% to account for the 5% reflection fee and allow the swap mechanism to go through.
